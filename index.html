ABOUT US

Secured Community is the private social network apps for you which connects your neighbourhood and your community. It's the easiest way for exploring your locality and communicate to your neighbours through forums and make all of your lives better in the real world residence. And moreover we are providing it for free.

People are using Secured Community to
* To have their locality’s announcements in their mobile.
* To connect with service providers around your locality.
* To place complaint to management or committee.
* To discuss with neighbourhood through forums.
* To inform the visitor details to security in advance.
* To check their maintenance bill status and keep remainder.

How it works

We strongly recommend our Secured Community Apps to all condominium, apartments or any gated community so that all your residents can stay connected with you always. We request management (or) committee of all condominium/apartment to contact us through our email contactus@securedcommunity.com or our Contact Us form.

We will be building an apps for your condominium/apartment, personalised with your condominium/apartment name and logo. We also provide a web application for the management (or) committee to administrate the users of your apps.

Notice Board

Notice board display list of notices or announcements posted by condo/apartment/any gated community management.

ServiceProvider

Display the list of service providers associated with the particular condo/apartment/any gated community or around your locality.

Forums

Residents or owners of condo/apartment/any gated community can communicate/discuss with the neighbours through this forum section.

Facility Booking

Booking of BBQ area, Multipurpose Hall or any available facility booking can be done from here. Complaints can also be placed.

Visitor Entry

Can track the visitor’s entry and exit. Also send notification to security in prior.

Others

Check the maintenance bill status. Condo rules can be displayed. Agreement details can be stored.
